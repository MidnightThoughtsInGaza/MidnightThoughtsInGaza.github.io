ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Mohammed Al-Bardawili is a writer from Gaza, born on the 23rd of November 1998. He holds a bachelor's degree in English Literature and has dedicated more than five years of his life to the field of digital marketing. During the war on Gaza, he experienced displacement from Gaza city (north), first to Rafah and later to Khan Younis. His personal experiences are deeply reflected in his writings, where he expresses the challenges of life under difficult circumstances and the hope for a better future.

BOOK DESCRIPTION

Midnight Thoughts in Gaza is a book characterized by deep simplicity and emotional richness, taking you on a journey into the mind of those who lived through the genocide in Palestine. Through a diverse collection of thoughts and reflections penned by the author from October 7th, 2023, to October 7th, 2024, the book offers a profound insight into the feelings of individuals confronted with the horrors of war. It provides a genuine glimpse into the ordeals faced by the people of Gaza during that devastating period and reveals the author's personal opinions and beliefs, reflecting the impact of these experiences on his thinking. Midnight Thoughts in Gaza serves as a window into internal conflicts and human experiences amidst times of war, offering readers a deeper understanding of the suffering of individuals and communities in the face of major crises.
